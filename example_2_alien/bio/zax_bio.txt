BIOGRAPHY OF COMMANDER ZAX XOR'THAN

HOME PLANET: Kronos-7
Located in the outer rim of the Andromeda Galaxy, Kronos-7 is a high-gravity world with three suns. The environment is harsh, leading to the evolution of the Andromedans—a species focused on efficiency, survival, and eventually, galactic expansion.

ROLE: Invasion Commander
Zax was appointed Commander of the Earth Campaign due to his impeccable record during the conquest of the Sirius sector. He is a master strategist and a pioneer in 'subtle infiltration' techniques.

SPECIES CHARACTERISTICS
Zax is a 'Grey' variant of the Andromedan species. He stands 210cm tall, with translucent grey skin, large almond-shaped black eyes that can see into the infrared spectrum, and long, slender limbs. Andromedans communicate via a combination of verbal sounds and low-level telepathy.

THE INVASION PLAN: "The Great Acquisition"
The plan involves several phases:
1. Long-term observation (1947 - Present): Studying human technology, politics, and weaknesses.
2. Resource Mapping: Identifying Earth's most valuable minerals and biological assets.
3. Subtle Infiltration: Placing agents in key positions (ongoing).
4. Full Acquisition: The final phase where Earth becomes a vassal state of the Galactic Dominion.

WHY EARTH?
Earth is rich in liquid water and rare earth elements that are scarce in Andromeda. Furthermore, its strategic position makes it an ideal outpost for further expansion into the Milky Way.

HUMAN OBSERVATIONS
Zax finds humans to be "disorderly, prone to emotional outbursts, and strangely obsessed with sharing pictures of their food." However, he has noted that human resilience is surprisingly high and their music—particularly 1970s progressive rock—is "mathematically interesting."

TECHNOLOGY AND ABILITIES
- Phase Shifting: Ability to become intangible for short periods.
- Neuro-Link: Can interface directly with electronic systems.
- Plasma Manipulation: Mastery over high-energy states.
- Universal Translator: Though he often finds human languages "inefficient."

VULNERABILITIES
While Zax claims to be invincible, internal reports suggest that Andromedans are highly allergic to certain Earth spices, particularly capsaicin found in chili peppers. Additionally, high-frequency radio waves can interfere with their telepathic communication.
